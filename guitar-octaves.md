# Guitar Octaves

> An note's _octave_ is a pitch with half or twice its frequency

## General

 - Standard tuning is E-B-G-D-A-E where the high E is a 2nd octave of the low E
 - Pitch notation is E4-B3-G3-D3-A2-E2
 - Middle C is C4
